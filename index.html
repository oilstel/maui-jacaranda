Maui Jacaranda

Season Map Places

The jacarandas are currently in bloom on Maui.

Each spring the non-native jacaranda trees bloom. The bloom's hotspot is in Kula, on the slopes of Haleakalā. If the background of this website is violet then the jacarandas are blooming. Use this map to visit a tree near you.
